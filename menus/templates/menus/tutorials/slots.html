How to Play Slots

Slot machines are one of the simplest and most popular forms of gambling in the world. With each spin, the machine will display three random symbols to you. Digits 0 through 9, $, *, and X are the possible symbols with our slot machines. Your payout depends on two factors: the symbols you get and a random multiplier chosen before each spin. If you get extra lucky, you could get two, three, four, or even five times the standard payout!

Here's how the payout is calculated:

  • If you get two matching numbers, your payout increases 5x.
  • If all three symbols are matching numbers, your payout increases 20x.

  • If you get one $, your payout is doubled.
  • If you get two $, your payout is increases 10x.
  • If all three symbols are $, your payout is increased 100x.

  • If you get two *, your payout increases 20x.
  • If all three symbols are *, your payout increases 50x.

  • However, if any symbol on the board is an X, you win nothing.

The above multipliers are then multiplied by the hidden value chosen at the start. This final multiplier is used to determine your payout.
